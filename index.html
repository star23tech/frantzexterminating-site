Questions

Common questions

Can you do a termite inspection for a home sale?

Yes. We provide wood-destroying insect (WDI) inspections and reports for buyers, sellers, and agents. Call as early as you can so we can fit the inspection to your closing date.

Do you offer ongoing plans, or just one-time treatments?

Both. We handle one-time problems, and we set up residential and commercial service plans that keep pests out on a regular schedule instead of waiting for the next flare-up.

What pests do you treat?

Ants, termites, mice, roaches, spiders, stink bugs, mosquitoes, and stinging insects like wasps and hornets, among others. If you're not sure what you're dealing with, describe it when you call and we'll help you figure it out.
